Participation by more than 40 officials from the Tanzania y Zanzibar Electoral Committees . According to the President of Zanzibar’s Electoral Committee, Jecha Salim Jecha, this initiative "will have a crucial impact on democratic reform at the level of electoral institutions and at the country level" (E87 PNUD).
