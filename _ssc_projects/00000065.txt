UNDP Mauritius  facilitated participation of Senior Government official/employment specialist from Finance Ministry in a high-level expert mission to share Mauritius experience with the Cote d’Ivoire Government and assist in developing an Operational Action-Plan to ensure tangible results, strengthen institutional coherence and the relevance of the Ivory Coast Government’s .  As a resulte of the study tour, Cote d"Ivoire  developed a policy on youth employment creation that was delivered at the level of the President.
