With help from UNDP, the Government of St. Lucia is in contact with the Mexican authorities to learn from the Mexican experience on multidimensional poverty.
A Memorandum of Understanding between UNDP and ECLAC was signed in order to join forces and share expertise; it has been confirmed that the next Forum will take place in 2015 in Peru as a joint initiative  .
