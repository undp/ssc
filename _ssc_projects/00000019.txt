UNDP in this regional initiative, facilitated knowledge exchange between the following countriesBurundi,Cameroon,Central African Republic,Democratic Republic of the Congo,São Tomé and Príncipe,Senegal.  The result of this  ECCAS-CEMAC regional initiative lead to the development of a policy document, “White Paper”, entitled “Regional Policy for Universal Access to Modern Energy Services and Economic and Social Development” which has been validated and adopted by the participating countries.
