UNDP facilitated  a five-day South-South knowledge exchange visit to Kenya for a Rwandan delegation  to meet with key partners supporting the the develpment of the Master's programme on Human Development at Kenyatta University.  The composition of the Rwanda mission included UNDP, Academia representatives and Government Representatives.Rwanda is currently considering introducing similar course in its national university.
