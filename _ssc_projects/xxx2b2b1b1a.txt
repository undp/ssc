Bangladesh and China established a multi-year partnership to exchange knowledge and practices on DRR and CC, opening new avenues for bilateral cooperation (China Plan 2013-15). The 6th Asian Ministerial Conference on DRRs established a declaration to integrate DRR and CCA into sustainable development goals and to strengthen regional cooperation (CBDM Eval 2014).
