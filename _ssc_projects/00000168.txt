After the April visit, Lam Dong (VNM) and Jambi (IDN) provinces have signed a MoU on 18 Sep 2014 to strengthen cooperation in the following areas  1)High Tech Agriculture Development 2)Exchange and share experience in forest management, protection and development, & other activities related to REDD+ 3)Exchange information & experts between the 2 provinces 4)Share and exchange agricultural techniques & methods (E111 MoU IDN-VNM; E112 IDN-VNM Visit)
