PIOJ met Chilean and Peruvian senior officials/management personnel from various Government agencies and was given presentations/field visits in management information systems, active labour market policies/programmes and cash/in-kind benefit transfer programmes. A Policy Brief on the Tour’s best practices is being developed as well as an online discussion forum on Best Practices & Institutional Frameworks for Poverty Reduction. A debriefing session with stakeholders is scheduled 1st qtr 2015
